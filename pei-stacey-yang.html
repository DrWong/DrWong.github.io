Pei (Stacey) Yang
Traditional Chinese Medicine Acupuncturist

Hello, I am Stacey, a licensed and board certified acupuncturist in Nova Scotia. As a practitioner, I am deeply passionate about holistic care and believe in the power of acupuncture to promote balance and vitality. My approach to acupuncture is rooted in a deep understanding of the interconnectedness of the mind, body, and spirit. I am committed to providing a safe and comfortable experience for each patient, promoting a relaxing and healing atmosphere throughout the treatment sessions. I strongly believe in fostering a collaborative partnership with my patients, empowering them to take an active role in their own healing journey. I look forward to meeting you and working together to optimize your health and well-being through the ancient art of acupuncture.

Languages Spoken: English and Mandarin Chinese
