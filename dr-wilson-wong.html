Dr. Wilson Wong 黄医生
Chiropractor

Hello and Welcome to Eaglewood Chiropractic Health Centre! People call me *Dr Wilson*, *Dr Wong* or simply *Wilson*. My post-secondary adventures include a Combined Honours in Biology and Neuroscience from Dalhousie University and a Doctor of Chiropractic from the Anglo-European College of Chiropractic in England.

I typically use a combination of trigger point, acupressure, active release, soft tissue techniques and supplementary and home therapies with chiropractic adjustments during treatments.

I returned to my hometown of Halifax to practice and to be close to family and I've been happily helping patients feel better since 2012. Although I enjoy treating patients, my favourite phrase is *see you when I see you*.

Languages Spoken: English, Cantonese Chinese and Mandarin Chinese
